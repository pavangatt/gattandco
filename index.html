What We Offer

Services designed around
every elder's needs

From daily companionship to medical support, our range of services ensures every individual receives the care and respect they deserve.

Companion Care

Regular visits from a dedicated buddy who offers conversation, emotional support, and genuine companionship to reduce loneliness.

Daily Living Assistance

Help with everyday tasks like meal preparation, light housekeeping, grooming assistance, and managing daily routines with dignity.

New

Elder-Friendly Home Setup

We assess and modify your home for maximum safety, mobility, and comfort — grab bars, ramp access, lighting, and bathroom safety upgrades.

Medical Appointment Support

Coordination, accompaniment, and follow-up support for doctor visits, therapy sessions, and health check-ups.

Medication Reminders

Timely, consistent reminders and gentle supervision to ensure elders adhere to prescribed medication schedules safely.

Nutrition & Meal Planning

Balanced, age-appropriate meal planning and preparation considering dietary restrictions, preferences, and health conditions.

Night-In Care

A caring buddy stays overnight for elders who need nighttime reassurance, assistance, or simply the comfort of knowing someone is there through the night.

Outings & Social Engagement

Safe, supervised outings to parks, community events, and social activities that keep elders engaged, active, and connected.

Home Safety Programme

Make your home
elder-friendly

Better mobility, safer spaces, and greater independence — we transform everyday homes into environments where seniors thrive with confidence.

Grab Bars & Handrails

Strategically installed in bathrooms, corridors, and stairways to prevent slips and support steady movement.

Ramp & Threshold Access

Smooth transitions at doorways and step-free ramp installations for wheelchairs and walkers.

Lighting Enhancements

Motion-sensor and night lighting for corridors, bathrooms, and staircases for safe navigation at all hours.

Furniture & Layout Adjustments

Rearranging furniture and recommending ergonomic aids to create obstacle-free, comfortable living spaces.

Who We Are

Rooted in compassion,
driven by purpose

Gatt & Co. was founded with a single belief: every elder deserves to age with dignity, independence, and the warmth of genuine human connection.

We are a Bengaluru-based elderly care organisation that partners with families to provide reliable, heartfelt, and professional buddy care services — right in the comfort of home.

Our team is carefully selected, trained, and matched to each individual — ensuring compatibility, trust, and a care experience that truly feels like family.
